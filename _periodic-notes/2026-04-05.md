## New KB Note

- [[Outcome Agent Architecture]] — Three-layer architecture for building custom, outcome-focused AI agents. Layer 1: Knowledge Store (persistent memory, chunked and separate from the model). Layer 2: Agent Recipes (pre-wired workflows producing editable artifacts — the modern punch card). Layer 3: Scheduling Loop (autonomous runs that feed learnings back into the Knowledge Store, compounding context over time). Maps cleanly to three principles: persistent memory, editable artifacts, compounding context. Open-source implementation: Open Brain. Cross-linked with [[Agentic Primitives]] (building blocks that map into this system), [[AI Second Brain Architecture]] (Layer 1 in practice), [[Twelve-Factor Agents]] (engineering discipline for Layers 1-2), and [[Commitment Loops]] (why artifacts > chat responses).

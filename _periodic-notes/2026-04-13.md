Includes the reactive-to-agentic progression (Zone 1 Foundational → Zone 2 Intermediate → Zone 3 Advanced), Sensei skill refiner (Ralph loop for description optimisation), and connection to our ADC/RPI work — our artefacts are structurally equivalent to Custom Skills.
